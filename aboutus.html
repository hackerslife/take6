Company Profile

Goyal Textiles is one of the most popular names in textile industry that is based in New Delhi with promoters having in-depth market experience in this field.Started with an aim to make accessible a variety of fabrics under a single roof to the market, we are working towards it and expanding our range with each passing year. Presently, we offer a unique collection of fabrics including Cambric, Canvas, Dobby, Drill, Twill, Voile, Interlinking and many more. In order to retain quality of fabrics for longer time, we use tamper proof and customized packaging materials. We handle and manage a well-equipped warehousing unit, wherein, personnel use advanced material handling equipment easily load, unload and move goods from one place to another. Also, the whole collection is stocked categorically for easy retrieval of products.
